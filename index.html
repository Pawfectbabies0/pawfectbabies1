About PawfectBabies

At Pawfectbabies Cattery,we dedicated to ethical breeding and raising well-socialized, healthy kittens. Our mission is to bring joy and companionship to families with our adorable Scottish Fold and Maine Coon kittens. we believe every family deserves the joy of a healthy, well-socialized, and truly exceptional kitten. As a TICA-registered cattery, we specialize in breeding Scottish Fold and Maine Coon kittens with the highest standards of care, ethics, and love. We proudly reside in Knoxville, Tennessee, and New York, allowing us to serve a wide community of pet lovers.
